# Facial Puffiness Mitigation — Habits Checklist

## Daily (High Impact)
- [ ] Hydrate steadily across the day (not front- or back-loaded)
- [ ] Prioritize potassium-rich foods (potatoes, leafy greens, legumes, avocado)
- [ ] Keep sodium intake consistent day-to-day (avoid spikes)
- [ ] Eat salty / carb-heavy meals earlier, not at night
- [ ] Walk 10–30 min within 1 hour of waking
- [ ] Maintain fixed wake time (±30 min)
- [ ] Get morning light exposure (5–15 min outdoors)
- [ ] Sleep with neutral neck alignment (no chin-to-chest flexion)

## After High Salt / Cured Meats
- [ ] Increase water intake for next 6–12 h
- [ ] Add potassium-first meal or snack
- [ ] Avoid lying flat post-meal
- [ ] Skip sauna / aggressive sweating

## After Alcohol
- [ ] Hydrate + electrolytes (potassium, magnesium; minimal sugar)
- [ ] Go to bed earlier than usual
- [ ] Cold face rinse in morning (cosmetic)
- [ ] Walk before caffeine

## Supplements (If Used)
- [ ] Creatine taken in the morning, not evening
- [ ] Magnesium (200–400 mg) in evening
- [ ] Omega-3s taken consistently (not episodically)
- [ ] Avoid diuretic blends and "water loss" stacks

## Sleep & Posture
- [ ] Slight head elevation (small angle only)
- [ ] Avoid thick pillows causing neck flexion
- [ ] Prefer side or slight tilt over fully supine
- [ ] Nasal breathing during sleep (habitual)

## Short-Term Cosmetic Tools (Optional)
- [ ] Cold compress / ice roll
